Chongqing Noodles


A Fiery Symphony of Flavors

Nestled in the heart of China's mountainous southwest, Chongqing is a city famed for its bold cuisine, misty landscapes, and unapologetic love for spice. Among its culinary treasures, Chongqing Xiaomian (重庆小面) stands out as a humble yet iconic dish that captures the essence of the city's fiery spirit. More than just a bowl of noodles, it is a cultural emblem—a daily ritual for locals and an unforgettable experience for visitors.
For Chongqingers, Xiaomian is more than breakfast—it's a way of life. The dish's affordability and speed make it a staple for office workers, students, and late-night revelers alike. Its addictive mala flavor even inspired a local saying: “No spice, no joy.”

A Bite of History

Chongqing noodles trace their roots to the city's bustling docks and working-class neighborhoods. Historically, laborers along the Yangtze River needed quick, affordable, and energizing meals to fuel their long days. Thus, Xiaomian was born—a simple dish of wheat noodles tossed in a punchy, aromatic sauce. Over time, it evolved into a beloved street food, with each vendor adding their own twist to the recipe. Today, it's a symbol of Chongqing's gritty charm and culinary ingenuity.

Step-by-Step


STEP1:Prepare the Seasoning Base

In a large bowl, combine the following ingredients:
Chopped pickled mustard tuber (榨菜末) 2 tables poons chili oil (辣椒油)
2 tables poons light soy sauce (生抽) Minced ginger and garlic (姜蒜末)
Chopped green onions (葱花) A pinch of Sichuan pepper powder (花椒粉)
Toasted white sesame seeds (白芝麻) A small amount of lard (猪油)
Salt (盐) and chicken powder (鸡精) to taste


STEP2:Noodles and Vegetables

1.Bring a pot of sufficient water to a boil.
2.Add noodles and green vegetables to the boiling water.
3.Cook until the noodles are tender and the greens are vibrant.


STEP3: Assemble the Dish

1.Once cooked, drain the noodles and vegetables, then transfer them directly into the prepared seasoning bowl.
2.Mix thoroughly to dissolve the lard and evenly coat the noodles with the flavorful sauce.
3.Top your bowl with a crispy fried egg for extra richness and texture!
